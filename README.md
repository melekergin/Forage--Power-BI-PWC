## Insights
Gender Distribution Across Job Levels:

- There are fewer women in executive roles, which suggests a need for targeted development programs to support women's career progression.

- Men are promoted at a higher rate than women, it may indicate a need to review and adjust promotion criteria to ensure fairness since performance Ratings are almost equal 

## Summary

- Representation:  The gender distribution across job levels helps identify if there are any levels where one gender is underrepresented. Addressing these imbalances can help create a more inclusive workplace.
- Promotion Equity:  The promotion rates by gender provide insights into whether promotions are being awarded equitably. Ensuring fair promotion practices can help retain top talent and foster a culture of meritocracy.
- Performance Evaluation:  The performance ratings by gender help assess if there are any biases in performance evaluations. Fair and unbiased evaluations are crucial for employee satisfaction and career development.

These insights can guide the organization in identifying areas for improvement in their diversity and inclusion efforts, ensuring fair representation, promotion, and evaluation processes for all employees.
